Frequently Asked Questions

🗳️ How do I register as a new voter?

Citizens who are 18 years or older can register by filling out Form 6 on the NVSP portal or through the Voter Helpline App.

📍 How can I find my polling booth?

You can use the "Know Your Polling Station" feature on the ECI website by entering your EPIC number or searching by details.

📝 Can I correct details in my Voter ID?

Yes, use Form 8 for any corrections (name, age, address, or photo) in your existing electoral entry.

🆔 What is an EPIC card?

EPIC stands for Elector's Photo Identity Card. It is the official identity document issued by the ECI to all eligible voters.
