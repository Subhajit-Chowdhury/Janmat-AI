The Voter Journey

Applying from:
1

Voter Registration (Form 6)

What: Formal application to join the Indian Electoral Roll.

Why: To exercise your constitutional right and receive your official EPIC (Voter ID) card.

How: Apply online. For Rural areas, your local BLO will coordinate with the Gram Panchayat for verification.

Visit Official ECI Portal: https://voters.eci.gov.in/
2

Verification & Roll Check

What: Identity authentication by a Booth Level Officer (BLO).

Why: To ensure the integrity of the database and confirm your polling station assignment.

How: The BLO visits your residence. In Rural areas, you can also consult your Pradhan's office for status.

Official ECI Search Portal: https://voters.eci.gov.in/voter/search
3

Casting Your Vote

What: Participating in the General or Assembly Elections.

Why: To shape the future of Bharat through your democratic power.

How: Locate your booth and carry a valid ID. In Rural areas, check the local primary school or community hall.

Polling Station Finder: https://voters.eci.gov.in/voter/polling-station

🗳️ What is Form 6?

Form 6 is for NEW voter registration. If you are 18+ and not registered, this is your starting point.

📝 What is Form 8?

Form 8 is for corrections (Name, Address, Photo) or shifting your constituency.

📍 Finding your Booth?

Use your EPIC number on the 'voters.eci.gov.in' portal to find your exact polling station.

🆔 Digital Voter ID?

New voters can download their e-EPIC (digital Voter ID) directly from the NVSP portal.
